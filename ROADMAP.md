## Next proposed release — v0.1.140

**Workspace Layout and Resize Foundation** must be planned and approved before implementation. The proposed scope is:

- Controlled widget size presets
- Supported cross-column placement
- Per-user saved sizes and positions
- Keyboard-accessible move and resize controls
- Layout reset and validation
- Preservation of native WordPress Screen Options and third-party widgets

5. Creator Mode and learning paths
